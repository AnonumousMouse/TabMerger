Restoring behaviour:
This applies to both group and global actions.
You can still manually delete:
  • tab → ⨯ button beside them (on the left)
  • group → Delete Group button (inside the group)
  • everything → global Delete All button
Opening a tab (Click on it) will remove that tab from TabMerger's page, the same goes for opening tabs in a group (Open Group button) and opening all tabs (Open All button).
Note: in the Open Group case, the corresponding (ONE) group will be removed. Likewise, in the Open All case, ALL groups will be removed.
Exclude Websites:

These must begin with "http://" or "https://" - we recommend copying the link of the tab and pasting it here.
